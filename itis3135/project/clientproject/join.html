Why Join 616 Charlotte?

Community of Fans

Joining 616 Charlotte means joining a space where you're not just another student — you're part of a team. Whether you're a longtime comic reader, MCU movie buff, or totally new to the Marvel Universe, there's a place for you here!

You'll get to meet other UNC Charlotte students who share your interests, geek out over your favorite characters, and create connections that go beyond the multiverse.

Creative Expression

Got a cool fan theory about Kang? Made a Miles Morales cosplay? Wrote a WandaVision-inspired poem? 616 Charlotte encourages creative expression in all forms. Share your work on:

  • The website
  • Social media spotlights
  • Club-led projects

We welcome contributions from artists, writers, thinkers, and even chaos agents.

Leadership & Volunteering

Want to level up your involvement? We're always looking for:

  • Club Officers: Help with planning events, managing socials, and keeping the club organized. Great for resumes and leadership experience.
  • Volunteers: Be the friendly face at the door, help set up movie nights, design flyers, or assist during campus events.

Have a voice

This isn't a top-down kind of group — 616 Charlotte is powered by its members. You'll be able to:

  • Vote in weekly polls
  • Submit ideas for event themes, trivia categories, and collabs
  • Suggest content to be featured on the homepage or gallery

We love seeing what excites our members and shaping the club based on your feedback.
